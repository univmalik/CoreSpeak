# Privacy Policy for **French Verbs – Intermediate**

**Effective Date:** February 25, 2026  
**Developer/Controller:** Deeper Language (deeperlanguage@gmail.com)

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with the **French Verbs – Intermediate** mobile application (the "App").

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what the App does and does **not** collect and your choices.

---

## 1) What We **Do Not** Collect
The App has **no accounts**. We do **not** ask you to register, sign in, or provide personal details. We do not collect or store on our servers:

- Your name, email address, phone number, or postal address  
- Your precise (GPS) location  
- Your contacts or calendar  
- Photos, media, or files (beyond lesson and audio assets packaged with the App)  
- Biometric data  
- Any user-generated content uploaded to our servers (we have no backend)

We also do not integrate analytics or crash-reporting SDKs that would transmit your usage to us.

---

## 2) Data Stored **Locally** on Your Device
The App saves a small amount of data **only on your device** (e.g., via Android SharedPreferences) to power core features:

- Lesson progress, best quiz scores, completion flags  
- UI preferences (e.g., last opened lesson)  
- Audio playback state (e.g., last played lesson, playback position)

This local data is not transmitted to us. You can delete it by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App.

---

## 3) Permissions
The App requests the following permissions:

- **INTERNET** – open any external web content you choose to open  
- **ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE** – check network connectivity  
- **FOREGROUND_SERVICE** / **FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK** – run the audio player in the background so lessons continue playing when you switch apps  
- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS** – display a media playback notification (play/pause/skip controls) while audio is active

We **do not** request precise location permissions (e.g., ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION).

---

## 4) Web Content & External Links
Lessons are rendered locally in a WebView. Some lessons may contain links to external sites you choose to open. Those sites are operated by third parties with their own privacy practices. We are not responsible for their content or policies.

---

## 5) Audio Playback
Audio lesson files are bundled within the App and played locally on your device. No audio is recorded, captured, or transmitted. The foreground service and notification are used solely to provide media controls (play, pause, skip) while the app runs in the background.

---

## 6) Data Sharing
- We do **not** sell or rent your personal data.  
- We do **not** share locally stored progress, quiz scores, or preferences with third parties.  
- We may disclose information if required by law (e.g., to comply with a subpoena, court order, or legal process) or to protect rights, safety, or investigate fraud.

---

## 7) Data Security & Retention
- **Local data:** Stored on your device until you delete it (clear App data or uninstall).  
- We use reasonable safeguards within our control, but no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

---

## 8) Children's Privacy
This App is intended for a general audience and is **not** directed to children under 13 (or the relevant age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us to request deletion.

---

## 9) International Transfers
Third parties may process and store data on servers located outside your country. Their use of your information is governed by their privacy policies and applicable laws.

---

## 10) Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post updates within the App or on the store listing and revise the **Effective Date** above. Please review it periodically.
